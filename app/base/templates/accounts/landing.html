Tokeneer

How we help you work with your purchasers to make decisions faster.

  • Purchaser Enters Checkout Page

    When a ticket checkout page loads, relevant event and ticket details are captured and sent to your discord.

  • Details Available In Discord

    You can view the details on the tickets/event and click a yes or no button. Your decision to buy or pass will be sent directly to your purchasers browser.

  • Decision Sent To Purchaser

    Your purchaser's browser will receive your decision and launch a popup in the checkout page with the details. It will also change the tab color to green or red so they can easily see when you've made a decision, even with lots of open tabs.

  • Tickets Purchased

    Just like that, your purchaser will finish buying the tickets.
